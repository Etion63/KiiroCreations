Commission Process

Commission Process

This is the process I follow to complete commissions. Please review these steps before commissioning so you are familiar with my process. Its pretty simple, but I think it is important to share!

Pre Commission

Before we start, you are expected to review the Terms of Service. I will then send you a document asking for a signature of agreement, and I will also ask for a valid ID.


Beginning Process

Great we have started! I will immediatly add you to my queue! I will then ask for you to provide me with a reference for what you are looking for. This can be a drawing, pdf, or literally anything that you feel gets your idea across! Then I will start building your website template.


Website Template

Building the website template is the most labor intensive process. I will provide you with as many progress screenshots as possible so you have a better idea of where I am at and what I am doing. After this is complete I will send you a schematic, of which everywhere that you can add text will be labeled. You will then provide me with all of said text and then we are in the end game!


End Game

I will help you compare domain options and assist with purchase setup. Domain costs are your responsibility, they are generally a monthly fee. Rarely they can be purchased with a one time payment but I encourage you to avoid this option at all costs because generally it is very very expensive. ($4000+!!)


Post Launch

After launch, I will provide you with detailed explanations of each code file in hopes that it can provide you with some sort of confidence in handling minor updates on your own!
