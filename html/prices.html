My Pricing

How does my pricing work?

Pricing depends on page count, complexity, and requested features of your website.Please keep in mind that I do not pay for your domain ownership!

Pre-packaged deals

Standard Website - $1000+

Includes 3 pages total: One home page plus two additional pages of your choice.

Deluxe Website - $1750+

Includes 5 pages total: One home page plus four additional pages of your choice.

Mega Website - $2000+

Includes 7 pages total: One home page plus six additional pages of your choice.

Build your own package

If you would rather build your own package I can totally work with you for that! The project will start as a Standard package and each additional page will cost an additional $400.

Other important details

  • Theming: Theme alone usually does not change price unless it is uber complex, in which case it will.
  • Art/Illustrations: You need provide art assets. If I coordinate directly with an artist, extra communication time may affect pricing. It goes without saying that you can totally provide art you make yourself!
  • Maintenance: Service requests are $75. Unlimited monthly updates are $50/month for minor edits and added content. If you instead choose to completely change your website, I will likely build it from the ground up for half the price of the original commission.

I aim to keep pricing as affordable as possible while delivering a fully custom site. Please enquire with any questions you have with my pricing!
